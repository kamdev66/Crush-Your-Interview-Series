WHat is referencing in mongodb?
:- referencing stores the relations between the data by including links.
:-By storing the ID of one document ,to another document ,it can form the relation.

What is embedding in mongodb?
:- embedding is the technique to store related data in a single document structure.
:-we can store these types of documents as sub-documents in MongoDB.We can either store them as an array or as an object.
