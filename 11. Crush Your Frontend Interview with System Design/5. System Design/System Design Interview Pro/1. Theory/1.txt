Ques:- What is System Design

Answer:-
System Design is the process of designing the elements of a system,
such as the architecture,modules and components,the different 
interfaces of those components.

There are two types of system design:-
1. LLD
2. HLD

Diff. between LLD and HLD?
LLD:-
1. Describes the design of each element mentioned in the High level
design of the system.
(jo hamne HLD ke andar elements likhe hai uski design ko describe krenge)
2. Classes,interfaces,relationships between different classes,and
actual logic of various components.

HLD:- 
1. Describes the main components that would be developed for the 
resulting product.
(agr hame resulting components bname hai to use main(mukhya)
 components HLD pr banne hai)
2. the system architecture details ,database design,services and 
processes,relationship between various modules and features.
